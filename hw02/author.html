The Grim Brothers

by Jacob Grimm and Wilhem Grimm

The Grimm brothers, Jacob Grimm and Wilhem Grimm, were born in 1785 and 1786, respectively.

They were German academics, philologists, cultural researchers, lexicographers and authors who together collected and published folklore during the 19th century. (https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Brothers_Grimm)

A lot of their stories take a dark turn, but there are different adaptations of them that appeared over the years.

Some notable stories of theirs include but are not limited to:

Cinderella

Death's Messengers

Hansel and Gretel

Iron John

Rapunzel

The Griffin
