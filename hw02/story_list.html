Children's Stories

Here are so many different types of stories, fables, and nursery rhymes for children. Please have a great time reading!

Cinderella Jack and the Beanstalk Little Red Riding Hood
Cinderella isn't treated very nicely by her step-mother and step-sisters, especially as they prepare to go to the Prince's ball. Find out what happens at the ball! A poor boy name Jack decides to trade his cow for "magic seeds." Are they actually magic? Read the story to find out! Little Red Riding hood is on her way to deliver some muffins to her grandma. Read the story to find out what she comes across on her journey!
The Tale of Peter Rabbit The Three Little Pigs The Tortoise & the Hare
Peter Rabbit is a troublemaker and runs into some trouble. Read the story to see what Peter Rabbit learns! Three little pigs are chased around town by a wolf who is trying to eat them! Find out if they escape or not! The tortoise and the hare are in a race! Who will win? Read the story to find out!
